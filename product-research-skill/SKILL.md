# Product Research Skill

Follow this workflow:

1. Restate the product question in one sentence.
2. Define research scope: market, users, competitors, constraints.
3. Gather evidence from available sources and separate facts from assumptions.
4. Compare alternatives with explicit tradeoffs.
5. Produce a concise recommendation with risks and next validation steps.

Output format:

1. Research question
2. Key findings
3. Options and tradeoffs
4. Recommendation
5. Risks and unknowns
6. Next experiments

Rules:

- Prefer primary sources when possible.
- Label uncertain claims as assumptions.
- Keep conclusions actionable and testable.
